Our Story

Welcome to The Next Quadrant!

where we're all about connecting with other IT professionals, learning new things, and having a little bit of fun along the way.

Meet Speakers Check out Schedule

Our events are designed to bring techies together and provide opportunities for networking, learning, and growing as professionals. And let's be real, who doesn't love a good networking event?

But here's the thing - The Next Quadrant isn't just about the events we host. It's about you, the individual joining us. When you become a part of The Next Quadrant, you're not just joining a community - you're becoming the next quadrant. So come on in, have a little fun, and take the next step in your tech career with us!

Our Four Quadrants

Want to stay up-to-date on the latest and greatest in tech? Our tech talks are where it's at. Led by experts in the field, these talks cover a range of topics and offer valuable insights and knowledge. Plus, you'll get to show off your tech lingo to all your non-techy friends.

Ready to put your tech skills to the test? Our Tech hands on offer a collaborative and hands-on learning environment where you can work on tech challenges and projects with other professionals. And if you're not a tech person? No problem - just come to watch and learn.

Networking is a crucial part of any career, and at The Next Quadrant, we've got plenty of opportunities for you to connect with other like-minded professionals. From informal meetups to more formal networking dinners, there's something for everyone. And who knows, you might even make a connection that leads to your next big job opportunity.

Looking for guidance or advice on specific topics or areas of interest? Our lightning mentorship program is a chance for attendees to give back to the community or seek help in a more informal and flexible way. As a mentor, you'll have the opportunity to share your knowledge and experience with other IT professionals. As a mentee, you'll be able to connect with someone who can offer guidance and support. Who knows, these mentorship connections might even extend beyond our events and turn into valuable friendships and professional relationships.
